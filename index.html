Strategy

Create Awareness


We go and talk to the people of the locality and tell them about the importance of basic education for their child.

Evaluate and Improve


Being effective in long term needs constantly examining our impact with questions about our methods and our people.

Quantify the Problem of Each Child


And accodingly admit them in appropriate classes.

Work on Scale


Our syllabus for each class is well defined accoding to the needs of that particular class's students.

Involve Volunteers


From the freshers' batch of IIIT-A to make sure that every child get equal attention.

Build Public Relations


With Alumni of IIIT-A, interested faculty and like-minded people for the financial needs of the initiative.
